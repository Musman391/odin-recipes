Frozen Peanut Butter Cheesecake

Description

This recipe will help you create the best peanut butter cheesecake that you have ever had. It consists of a buttery texture and flavor combined with the best thing ever "Peanut Butter".

Using this recipe you should be able to create a delicious desert for yourself as a treat or for other people coming over to your place, either way it's a blast to create and eat

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 1/2 cups crispy rice cereal
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (10 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup chocolate fudge sauce
